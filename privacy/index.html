Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Dealer Sensei is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers or any third parties.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Dealer Sensei does not require an account and does not collect personal information for transmission to the developer. The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Local Storage

Dealer Sensei stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Gameplay settings and preferences
  • Training history and progress
  • Streak data and achievements
  • Table rules configuration

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the app, this data is removed.

In-App Purchases

Dealer Sensei offers optional in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit framework. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Dealer Sensei does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. The only external communication is through Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchase verification.

Children's Privacy

Dealer Sensei does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is a single-player educational game with no social features, accounts, or data collection.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new policy and update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

support@dealersensei.com
