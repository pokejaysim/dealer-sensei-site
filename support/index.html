Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dealer Sensei?

Dealer Sensei is a blackjack game that teaches you basic strategy while you play. It provides real-time coaching, showing you the mathematically optimal play for every hand, so you can learn to play smarter at the casino.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No. Dealer Sensei works completely without an account. Just download and start playing — all your progress is saved locally on your device.

Does the app collect my data?

No. Dealer Sensei does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All gameplay data and progress are stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What does PRO include?

PRO unlocks advanced training tools, the PRO Lab, and additional features to help you master basic strategy faster. It's a one-time purchase — no subscriptions.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes! Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your PRO access will be restored.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Dealer Sensei works completely offline. No internet connection is needed to play, train, or access any features.

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your progress in the app's Settings menu. Note that this action cannot be undone.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at support@dealersensei.com with a description of the issue and your device model/iOS version. Screenshots are helpful!
